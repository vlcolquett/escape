Escape

The warrior princess is missing and none of you seem to care... pity. Well, I retrieved her and I have broken a few rules, but I do not think you all will mind... While this page has some clues so does the living room. Don't worry, there's nothing beyond the living room. Think of this as an escape room, but you're breaking out the warrior princess.

You should not have to break, tear, or damage anything to make progress...

I am sure you can brute force some of puzzles and even hack this webpage; do what you want. In my opinion, one way is fun and the other is lame...

You can always ask for help or clarification... doesn't mean I will give it to ya...

Have fun! I have already had lots!
